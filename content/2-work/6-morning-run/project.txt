Title: Morning Run

----

Text: 

This TVC is the first in an integrated campaign for Nikolovski Lawyers. Along with the other commercials, [The Fighter][3] and [Family Building][2], this campaign also featured bus and newspaper advertising, featuring stills from each scene.

Shot on RED EPIC-X with Canon CN-E Primes. For a full behind-the-scenes look at this production, check out [the blog][1].
